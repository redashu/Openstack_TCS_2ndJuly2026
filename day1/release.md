## Common Interview Questions

### How many OpenStack releases occur each year?

Two (one around April and one around October).

### Why are releases named alphabetically?

To provide an easy-to-follow release sequence. Historically, names were chosen by the community and often referenced locations near OpenStack Design Summit venues.

### What is the latest upstream release?

2026.1 (Gazpacho), released on 1 Apr 2026.
